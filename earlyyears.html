Trinton's early years

Born June 3rd 2006 In Tokyo Japan

Trinton joined baseball little league with elementary school friends.He joined just as a hobby like most kids at that early age,but soon became to love the game. As he showed more interest in the sport,his parents became more involved in his body training,baseball practices and games.

Weekend games and baseball practice became apart of the family schedule. Besides school work studying, All other time was focused on baseball in some form or fasion.

Trinton decided very early on that he wanted to play Major League Baseball

His family saw his passion for baseball and supported him 100%.Anything he needed for training or practice his parents provided
