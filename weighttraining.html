Trinton's Weight Training

At an early age developing Trinton's muscles was very important in giving him an advantage over the other kids his age.

Trinton and his father would begin arm strenthening to give im enough power to make the throw from home to second base with no bounce as his position as catcher.

As he began to throw more accurate and powerful,his coaches took notice of his arm strengh and accuracy then changed his position to pitching.

He continues his muscle training to this day,conditioning his body for Major league competition.

Trintons family felt it was important to have training equipment at home to train as much as possible,and to develop his muscles gradually and savely.
