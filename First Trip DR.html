Trinton's First Trip to The Dominican Republic

August Of 2019 Baseball training camp

Trinton and his father went to the DR in 2019 due to a connection of a Dominican native his father worked with,special thanks to Felix Reyes.

Training in the DR for Two weeks tought Trinton a different and more intense way of training.

He was able to focus on batting techniques and fielding drills that helped him improve his level of play.

Trinton also was able to meet other players his age and older from the DR who also has a passion for baseball as much as he does.
